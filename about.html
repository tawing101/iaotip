About suroyIAO

suroyIAO was born from a simple observation: the best parts of Siargao aren't found in a glossy brochure, but in the rhythm of the tides and the authentic local village vibes of General Luna.

Our Minimalist Mission

In an age of information overload, our mission is to provide a minimalist, essential guide for travelers who value quality over quantity. We don't list every business on the island—only the spots we truly love and visit ourselves. From the perfect morning espresso at Whitebeard to the sunset community sessions at Catangnan Bridge, we prioritize the "soul" of Siargao.

Live Island Intelligence

We believe that having the right data at the right time is the key to a perfect island day. That is why we integrate real-time maritime data for Cloud 9 and General Luna. By providing live tide status, swell heights, and wind speeds, we help surfers and adventurers make informed decisions, ensuring you spend less time scrolling and more time in the water.

Our Curation Process

Unlike massive travel directories that use automated bots, every recommendation on suroyIAO is hand-picked and human-verified. We focus on supporting local entrepreneurs, sustainable practices, and businesses that contribute to the unique culture of the island.

We believe that less is more. By simplifying your search for essential island information, we give you more time to actually experience the magic of Siargao.

suroyIAO is a locally-driven project dedicated to the surfers, the explorers, and the global travelers who call Siargao home for a while.
