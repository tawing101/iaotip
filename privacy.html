Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 07, 2026

At suroyIAO, we value the privacy of our island explorers. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require user accounts or personal login information. We may collect non-identifiable technical data such as browser type and device information to ensure the "Live Conditions" and "Poster Studio" tools function correctly on your device.

2. Use of Cookies

We use essential cookies to save your preferences, such as your cookie consent choice. We also use third-party services like Google AdSense to serve personalized ads based on your visits to this and other websites.

3. Third-Party Services

Our site includes links to external services like Google Maps and Open-Meteo for live weather data. These third parties have their own privacy policies regarding how they handle your interaction with their maps or data APIs.

4. Data Security

We do not store personal data on our servers. Any data generated via our tools (like your customized QR codes or posters) is processed locally in your browser and is not uploaded to us.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please reach out to us at info@suroyIAO.com.
